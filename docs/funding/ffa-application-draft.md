## Problem Statement

Hospitality operators in Africa run complex, high-risk businesses with fragmented tools. Critical operational data often sits across POS systems, spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, paper checklists, compliance folders, maintenance logs, and manual manager reports.

This creates five structural problems:

1. Executives do not see operational risk early enough.
2. General managers spend too much time compiling reports instead of acting.
3. Compliance and maintenance issues escalate silently until they become legal, guest experience, or revenue problems.
4. Labour and sales decisions are made reactively, often after the shift has already been lost.
5. Multi-site operators cannot compare site health consistently across brands, locations, and management teams.

Existing restaurant tools report metrics. ForgeStack is built to operationalise them: detect risk, prioritise action, and create an accountability loop.

## Solution

ForgeStack connects operational modules into a single AI-powered command centre:

- Sales intelligence and revenue pace monitoring
- Labour analytics and labour cost visibility
- Compliance tracking and audit readiness
- Maintenance workflows and escalation
- Inventory intelligence roadmap
- Oracle MICROS / POS integrations
- Head-office multi-site dashboards
- Real-time alerts and operational action queues
- GM co-pilot decision support
- Operating score and site health scoring

The platform gives each role the right view:

- GMs see what needs action today.
- Head office sees multi-site operational health.
- Executives see revenue, risk, compliance, and operational performance.
- Auditors and area managers see exceptions and accountability.

ForgeStack is built as a multi-tenant SaaS platform with tenant isolation, role-based access control, row-level security, structured logging, background job queues, and integration health monitoring.
