## Reusable Problem Statement Draft

African hospitality operators manage high-risk, multi-site businesses through fragmented systems. Critical data sits across POS platforms, maintenance logs, compliance records, spreadsheets, checklists, WhatsApp threads, and manager memory. This creates delayed visibility, weak accountability, inconsistent reporting, compliance exposure, maintenance failures, labour inefficiency, and lost revenue opportunities.

ForgeStack exists to turn those fragmented signals into a live operational layer that tells each operator what needs attention, what is at risk, what affects revenue, and what action should happen next.

## Reusable Solution Draft

ForgeStack is a multi-tenant operations intelligence platform built for restaurants, hotels, and other distributed physical operations. It combines operational command dashboards, compliance workflows, maintenance escalation, labour visibility, sales intelligence, alerting, and executive reporting into one governed environment. Instead of acting like a passive dashboard, ForgeStack is positioned as an operational command system that helps site managers and head office detect issues early, prioritise action, and maintain execution accountability.

ForgeStack's beachhead is South African hospitality: restaurant groups, hotels, and multi-site operators that need better compliance visibility, operational reporting, maintenance oversight, and cross-site management. From there, the expansion path extends into franchise groups, shopping centres, precinct operators, and broader enterprise physical operations across Africa.
